2018 Social Impact Hackathon

180 Degrees Consulting is proud to host our first-ever social impact hackathon.
Solve complex organisational problems for our client, Out For Australia,
meet talented students like yourself and make tangible social impact on October 20-21.

Introduction

Be part of a weekend of ideas, innovation and impact by participating in 180 Degrees Consultingâ€™s inaugural Social Impact Hackathon.

This Social Impact Hackathon will address inclusion of LGBTI+ students and professionals. Despite marriage equality and advances in LGBTIQ rights across Australia, research shows that only 32% of LGBT+people are out to everyone with whom they work, and this is even lower for young LGBT+ people.

The event will bring together talented and innovative students, industry mentors and representatives from Out for Australia, our not-for-profit client, to co-design a strategy for better strengthening the sense of community among LGBT+ professionals and students.

Launch: October 15

We will kick off the Hackathon with a 2 hour information session where we will talk through the challenge brief and help build your understanding of Out for Australia. Kick start the hackathon with a 2-hr session where you will hear from our Community Partners as they talk through the challenge statements. This would be the best opportunity for you to understand the challenge statements and the profile of the beneficiaries.
